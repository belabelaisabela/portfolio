Harry Potter

Harry James Potter is a fictional character protagonist of the eponymous series of books by British author JK Rowling. In the Brazilian translation, he received the full name of Harry Tiago Potter.

Eleven

Eleven, later known as Jane Hopper, is a fictional character in the Netflix television series Stranger Things. She is played by Millie Bobby Brown and was created by brothers Matt and Ross Duffer.

Joker

Isolated, intimidated and disregarded by society, failed comedian Arthur Fleck begins his path as a criminal mastermind after murdering three men in the middle of the subway.

Spider Man

Spider-Man, Peter Parker's alter ego, is a superhero who appears in comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer/editor Stan Lee and writer/artist Steve Ditko.

Walter White

Walter Hartwell White is a fictional character from the American drama television series Breaking Bad. He was created by Vince Gilligan and is played by Bryan Cranston.

Thomas Shelby

Thomas Michael Shelby is a fictional character and the main protagonist of the British period crime drama Peaky Blinders. He is played by Irish actor Cillian Murphy.

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the protagonist of the One Piece created by Eiichiro Oda. He is a young man whose body gains the properties of rubber after eating the Gomu-Gomu no Mi, a cursed fruit.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa is a character from Attack on Titan manga by Hajime Isayama. She is a villager who lives with Eren Yeager, and his family who took her in after her parents died in a kidnapping attempt.
